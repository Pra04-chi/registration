PROFILE

Established in 1999, located in the heart of the city of Yamuna Nagar (Haryana), TIMT is a member of the glorious family of Seth Jai Parkash Mukand Lal Institutions of Knowledge and Service that runs as many as 25 Institutions of repute in Northern India. It has been named after Prof. Tilak Raj Chadha, a living legend who persistently worked for the cause of morals and standards in education.
